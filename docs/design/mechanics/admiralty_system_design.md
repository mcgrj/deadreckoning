# The Admiralty System
## Meta-Progression, Narrative Framing, and Political Layer

---

# 🎯 Overview

The **Admiralty** is the between-run hub that unifies:
- progression
- narrative interpretation
- systemic variation
- world persistence

It is not a traditional upgrade screen.

> It is a political institution that interprets, rewards, and distorts the outcomes of your expeditions.

---

# 🧠 Core Design Goals

## 1. Provide Meaningful Progression
- Unlock new **options**, not raw power
- Expand decision space across runs

## 2. Reinforce Theme
- Authority, bureaucracy, and misinterpretation
- Tension between truth and report

## 3. Connect Runs Narratively
- Persistent ship logs
- Rumours and institutional memory

## 4. Introduce Controlled Variability
- Political events
- Resource fluctuations
- Changing priorities

---

# 🏛️ Core Loop (Between Runs)

1. Review ship log (what actually happened)
2. Submit official report (what you claim happened)
3. Admiralty evaluates and rewards
4. Spend influence/resources
5. Political events occur
6. Prepare next expedition

---

# 📖 Ship Log System

## Purpose
- Record the truth of the expedition
- Feed into future narrative and mechanics

## During Run
- Logs key decisions and outcomes

## After Run
- Generates summary:
  - tone reflects leadership style
  - highlights major events

---

# 🧾 Report System (Critical Mechanic)

## Player Chooses Narrative Framing

Examples:
- Emphasise discipline
- Blame crew
- Admit failure
- Conceal wrongdoing

---

## Effects

- Determines:
  - rewards
  - unlock paths
  - Admiralty perception

- May diverge from truth

---

# ⚖️ Admiralty Evaluation

The Admiralty has:
- biases
- doctrines
- priorities

---

## Example Preferences

- Values discipline → rewards authoritarian play
- Values results → rewards survival regardless of cost
- Values reputation → punishes scandal

---

## Outcome

Player gains:
- Influence (currency)
- Access to doctrines
- New unlock opportunities

---

# 🧱 Admiralty Departments

These replace traditional “buildings”.

---

## 1. Naval Doctrine Office

Unlocks:
- new cards
- leadership approaches

Examples:
- Strict Discipline Doctrine
- Shared Command Doctrine

---

## 2. Logistics Bureau

Unlocks:
- starting conditions
- supply variations

Examples:
- Better rations (higher theft risk)
- Reduced crew size (more control, less capacity)

---

## 3. Personnel Office

Unlocks:
- new crew archetypes

Examples:
- Zealot
- Smuggler
- Veteran

---

## 4. Records Office

Handles:
- ship logs
- expedition history

Feeds:
- persistent memory system
- future narrative variation

---

## 5. Political Oversight

Controls:
- what is rewarded
- what is suppressed

---

# 🎲 Political Event System

## Purpose
Introduce dynamic variation between runs.

---

## Types of Events

### Policy Changes
- “Increased pressure for results”
- Rewards survival over ethics

---

### Resource Shifts
- “Supply shortages”
- Start runs with fewer resources

---

### Political Pressure
- “Public scrutiny”
- Punishes reported failures more harshly

---

### Institutional Conflict
- Admiralty factions disagree
- Unlock competing doctrines

---

# 🔄 Effects on Gameplay

Political events influence:
- available upgrades
- starting conditions
- event probabilities
- crew behaviour

---

# 🧠 Progression Types

## 1. Knowledge Unlocks
- reveal hidden traits
- show system indicators

---

## 2. Cards
- new decision tools
- context-sensitive

---

## 3. Crew Archetypes
- new personalities
- new emergent dynamics

---

## 4. Doctrines
- rule modifiers
- shift gameplay style

---

## 5. Loadouts
- tradeoff-based starting advantages

---

# ⚖️ Design Rules

## Must:
- add variety
- preserve tension
- create tradeoffs

## Must Not:
- provide guaranteed success
- remove uncertainty
- create dominant strategies

---

# 🔥 Key Experience

The player should feel:

> “I survived something terrible… and now I must explain it to people who were not there.”

---

# 🧭 Final Identity

The Admiralty is:

- a progression system
- a narrative filter
- a political force

---

> It transforms your game from a series of runs into a living system of stories, power, and perception.
